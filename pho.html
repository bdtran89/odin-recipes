Ingredients:

  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 3-inch piece ginger, sliced
  • 3 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • Fresh basil, cilantro, and green onions
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Hoison sauce and Sriracha for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, toast the onion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon, and cloves until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef broth, fish sauce, sugar, and salt. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and rinse.
  4. Strain the broth to remove solids, then return the broth to the pot.
  5. To serve, divide the noodles into bowls, top with sliced beef, and pour the hot broth over to cook the meat.
  6. Garnish with bean sprouts, fresh herbs, lime, and sauces.
